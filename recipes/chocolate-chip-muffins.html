Chocolate Chip Muffins

Description

Start your day in the sweetest way: With this chocolate chip muffin recipe!

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Oil
  • Egg
  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Baking Powder
  • Salt
  • Chocolate Chips

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C)
  2. Combine milk, oil, and egg in a small bowl until well blended.
  3. Pour milk mixture into well and stir until batter is just combined; fold in chocolate chips.
  4. Spoon batter into the prepared muffin cups, filling each 2/3 full.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven until tops spring back when lightly pressed, about 18 to 20 minutes.
