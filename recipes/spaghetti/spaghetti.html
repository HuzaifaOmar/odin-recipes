spaghetti

Description

This million dollar spaghetti dish features a beefy, creamy tomato sauce that's impossible to resist.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Beef
  • Spaghetti sauce
  • Butter
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Boil the spaghetti in salted water, then drain and set aside.
  2. Cook the beef
  3. Prepare the baking dish, then spread half of the noodles in the bottom
  4. Cover with remaining spaghetti and butter.
  5. Pour the beef mixture over the spaghetti.
  6. Bake for 30 minutes, top with Cheese, and continue baking until the cheese is melted.
